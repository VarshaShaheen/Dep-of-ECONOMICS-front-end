Description

The MA Applied Economics programme offered by the department is a two year full time course comprising of four semesters. The curriculum is structured to give students a solid understanding of both the theoretical and practical concepts of economics. The course imparts a strong foundation on macroeconomic and microeconomic concepts along with knowledge regarding the nature and role of businesses. The students will also learn to evaluate organisational decisions and government policies with the help of basic economic principles and gain a range of transferable skills in task-planning, communication, research, information analysis, interpretation and team-working.
