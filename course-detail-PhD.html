Description

The Department started offering PhD degree programme from its inception. The programme is designed to develop students for careers in academia, industry, governments and research institutions. The admitted students are requiring to complete six months course work associated with doctoral research programme. The students can do research in the areas of real-world economic problems related to economics, issues in society with appropriate economic theory framework etc.
